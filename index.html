WELCOME LOSER!

Greetings adventurer! You've suddenly found yourself far away from your home in the south east of the kingdom! Now you have no choice but to brave your way through the wastelands to hopefully make it back home. Oh, did we mention you're the crown prince and need to get home for your coronation? Yeah, that's a bit important. Keep safe from the monsters that will try to kill you. Also have fun :^)

how to play

using the arrow or wasd keys on your keyboard, move your lost hero around the board so he can find his way home. Be wary of enemies as some are stronger than others! If you find yourself in a fight you can't win, running is an option (but only loser run ayy lmao)
ENEMY HP: 69

WARLORD ENDING

The hero came back just in time to find himself crowned king of the land as his father passed while he was away. Unbeknownst to those living in the kingdom, the newly crowned king found humself eager for more and more bloodshed which eventually led him to becoming infamous to kingdoms far and wide. His need to conquer brough him the title of the "Red King". No one outisde the kingdom could sleep well as they knew it was only a matter of time before they, too, fell to the Red King.

PACIFIST ENDING

As our hero found his way finally back home, he was crowned not prince, but as king as his father had passed in the time he had been away. Because of his peaceful nature and refusal to attack even the most frightening of opponents while outside the kingdom, he brought an end to his father's more warmonger-y ways and the once battle-hungry kingdom became peaceful for the first time in its history. However, this left them open for attack and only so many moons later they found themselves wishing for the war-hungry king of past...
